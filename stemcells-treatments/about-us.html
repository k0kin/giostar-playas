About Us

Giostar Mexico

At GIOSTAR Mexico, we are pioneers in the application of advanced stem cell technology to treat degenerative and genetic diseases. Our approach focuses on personalizing treatment protocols designed to enhance the body's natural healing abilities, positioning us as leaders in the field of regenerative medicine. With over twenty years of experience, our team of scientists and clinicians has been at the forefront of stem cell research and development. Throughout this time, we have continually expanded our therapeutic areas, offering innovative and personalized solutions that improve the quality of life for our patients. GIOSTAR Products and Services At GIOSTAR Mexico, we specialize in advanced stem cell treatments, focusing on two main therapeutic products: Stem Cell Treatment Protocols: These protocols are personalized for each patient and are designed to harness the body's natural healing potential. We use stem cells derived from various sources, such as bone marrow, adipose tissue, and umbilical cord. These treatments are effective for a wide range of medical conditions, from degenerative diseases to immunological disorders. NK (Natural Killer) Cell Treatment Protocols: These immune system cells are vital in the fight against malignant cells and pathogens. NK cell treatments are targeted at patients with oncological, immunological, and other critical conditions, enhancing the body's ability to combat diseases more effectively. Our protocols include: Vials of cells and exosomes Medical consultation Hyperbaric chamber or oxygen therapy Nutritionist Physical therapy We recommend reinforcement treatments every three months to maximize the benefits of the therapies. Services Our comprehensive approach to medical tourism ensures a hassle-free experience for our international patients. We offer a full range of logistical and medical services to facilitate every stage of the treatment process: Ground Transportation: We coordinate all aspects of local transportation, ensuring that our patients arrive at our facilities safely and comfortably. Accommodation: We manage our patients' stay in comfortable and secure accommodations, ensuring a relaxing and stress-free experience. Medical Care: We provide continuous and personalized medical care, from reception to post-treatment follow-up, adjusting protocols as needed to achieve the best possible results. Therapeutic Areas At GIOSTAR Mexico, we strive to improve the quality of life for our patients across a variety of medical conditions, organized into the following categories: Wellness and Anti-Aging: Protocols designed to enhance vitality, slow the aging process, and promote overall health. Pain Management: Treatments aimed at relieving chronic pain and improving patients' quality of life. Degenerative Diseases: Therapies for conditions such as osteoarthritis, heart disease, and other diseases that result in the degeneration of tissues and organs. Neurodegenerative Diseases: Innovative treatments for conditions like Alzheimer's, Parkinson's, and other neurodegenerative diseases. Immunological Diseases: Protocols to treat autoimmune disorders and enhance the body's immune response. Oncological Diseases: Advanced therapies for cancer treatment, including the use of NK cells to target cancer cells. Our Locations Los Algodones: Our first clinic in Mexico, opened in 2016, located within Mediplaza, ideal for accessing a variety of medical treatments, with amenities such as a hotel, restaurant, and pharmacy. Playa del Carmen: Opened in 2020, it is our clinic with the highest patient volume, located in a gated community with a stunning coastal environment. Cancun: Our newest clinic, currently in the process of launching. We are developing a positioning strategy to attract patients. Guadalajara: In partnership with Hospital San Javier, we offer cellular treatments supported by Dr. Carlos Rodriguez, although we are not currently accepting patients at this location. Commitment to Excellence GIOSTAR Mexico is revolutionizing medical tourism with cutting-edge stem cell treatments for degenerative and genetic diseases. Our commitment to patient-centered care ensures a seamless and stress-free experience through personalized logistical coordination and dedicated concierge services. Unlike other providers, GIOSTAR's holistic approach integrates nutrition, physical therapy, and complementary therapies to maximize recovery and overall well-being. We understand that each patient is unique, and we strive to offer a personalized experience that meets their needs and expectations.
